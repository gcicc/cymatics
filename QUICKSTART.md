# Cymatics — Quick Start Guide

## Install on Your Phone (Right Now)

### iPhone
1. Open Safari and go to: **https://gcicc.github.io/cymatics/**
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**
5. App appears on your home screen — tap to launch

### Android
1. Open Chrome and go to: **https://gcicc.github.io/cymatics/**
2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) at top right
3. Tap **Install app**
4. Confirm installation
5. App appears in your app drawer

## How to Use

### Basic Steps
1. **Adjust Frequency** — Use the slider (100–2000 Hz)
2. **Press Vibrate** — Particles scatter and tone plays for 6 seconds
3. **Watch** — Particles settle into geometric pattern based on frequency
4. **Change Theme** — Click Sand, Blue, Gold, or Neon to change colors

### Try These Frequencies
- **200 Hz** — Simple cross (+ pattern)
- **440 Hz** — Star pattern (musical A note)
- **660 Hz** — Hexagonal grid
- **880 Hz** — Radial spokes
- **1200 Hz** — Dense lattice

Or use the **Preset buttons** to jump straight to these patterns.

### Microphone Mode
1. Tap the **🎤 Mic Off** button
2. Allow microphone access when prompted
3. Hum or sing into your phone
4. Watch the pattern respond to your voice frequency in real-time
5. Tap again to disable microphone

### Controls
- **Frequency Slider** — Choose pitch (Hz)
- **Volume Slider** — Audio level (0–100%)
- **Presets** — 5 iconic patterns (200, 440, 660, 880, 1200 Hz)
- **Color Theme** — Visual style (Sand, Blue, Gold, Neon)
- **🎤 Mic Toggle** — Microphone input mode
- **Vibrate** — Start the simulation
- **Reset** — Randomize particles

## What You're Seeing

Each frequency creates a unique **Chladni pattern** — the sand settles into nodal lines where the plate has zero vibration. Higher frequencies = more complex patterns.

This is real physics (harmonic resonance), but simulated on your phone in real-time.

## Offline

Once loaded, Cymatics works **offline** — the app is cached on your device.

## Troubleshooting

**Particles aren't moving?**
- Try a different frequency or preset
- Check that your phone's volume is on

**Microphone not working?**
- Confirm you granted microphone permission
- Check phone Settings → Apps → Cymatics → Permissions
- Make sure there's ambient sound to detect

**App won't install?**
- Use Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS)
- Clear browser cache if issues persist
- Try again in a few seconds

## About Cymatics

Cymatics is the study of visible sound. When a surface vibrates at different frequencies, particles settle into patterns unique to that frequency. These patterns are called **Chladni figures**, discovered by Ernst Florens Friedrich Chladni in 1787.

Watching particles form these shapes makes the invisible vibrations of sound suddenly — deeply, viscerally — *visible*.
