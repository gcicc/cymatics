Cymatics

Cymatics is the study of visible sound. Watch particles of sand settle into intricate geometric patterns when exposed to different sound frequencies.

Each frequency creates a unique pattern—from simple crosses to complex hexagons. Press Vibrate to scatter the particles and watch them settle into the pattern.

Try different frequencies, use the presets, or use your microphone to see patterns respond to your voice!
